Squid

€ 6,-

This lovely small squid has been handcrafted with a 100% cotton and filled with a 100% wool and love.

The normal version measures around 5cm/2 inches tall and the top hat version measures around 8cm/3 inches tall.

The normal version is available in the colors green and purple, the top hat version is only in the pink and green colors available.

Octopus pattern by All From Jade and Top Hat pattern by Club Crochet.
